Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I restore my PRO purchase?

If you have purchased the PROductive subscription but still see ads or cannot access PRO features (like CSV export), you may need to restore your purchase:

Open the AccuRate app.

Go to the Settings screen (gear icon).

Tap the "Restore Purchases" button (if available) or tap the "Go PROductive" card again.

The app will check your Apple ID for existing subscriptions and unlock the features automatically. You will not be charged again.

2. How do I export my data?

CSV Export is a PRO feature.

Go to the History (Sessions) screen.

Tap the Share icon (top right corner).

Choose where you want to save or send your CSV file (e.g., Save to Files, Email, AirDrop).

3. I forgot to stop the timer. Can I edit a session?

Currently, sessions cannot be edited after they are saved. We are working on an "Edit Session" feature which will be available in the next update.

For now, you can delete the incorrect session and start a new one, or manually track the difference.
